Support & bookings

A charitable, low-pressure way to talk about your tech.

A "session" with byAIm is more like a conversation than a formal assessment. We talk about what your days actually look like, which bits of technology are tripping you up, and where AI might quietly help in the background.

Request a call Send a question

You never have to turn your camera on. Breaks are expected. "I'm too tired today" is a perfectly acceptable reason to reschedule.

How the charitable bit works

Instead of a fixed fee, we agree a suggested donation to an MS or neurological charity. If you already donate to a specific organisation, we can keep supporting them. If not, I can suggest a few options.

  • You choose the charity (or charities) that matter most to you.

  • For organisations, we can pair project fees with public or private donations.
What we can cover together

Sessions can be very focused or very open. Common themes:

  • Setting up AI tools to help with emails, forms and paperwork.
  • Organising files, photos and scans so you can actually find things.
  • Exploring voice control, shortcuts and one-handed-friendly setups.
  • Looking at your current devices and seeing what they can already do.

For organisations wanting a more structured discovery session, see the Business & Organisations page.
