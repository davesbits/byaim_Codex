About byAIm

Human-centred AI & IT support led by lived experience.

I'm Dave, living with multiple sclerosis and building byAIm as a charitable-first way to use AI, automation, and thoughtful IT to reduce friction for people and organisations who need tech to be kinder. Your donations to MS and neuro charities are the currency for the help I provide.

I focus on practical, lightweight changes: voice-friendly setups, gentle automations, clearer workflows, and realistic cloud choices that respect limited energy and time. Whether you're an individual, a small charity, or a team, the goal is the same: tech that quietly supports you instead of draining you.

What I bring
  • 30+ years across product, engineering, and IT with a bias for accessibility.
  • Hands-on experience living with MS, so accommodations are built-in, not bolted on.
  • Cloud, AI, and automation that stay maintainable for small teams and individuals.
  • Clear follow-ups and documentation so you can move at your own pace.
