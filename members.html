Members area

A quieter space for people living with MS and other neurological conditions.

The members area is designed for people who need technology to be gentle, predictable and energy-aware. Think of it as a calm dashboard where you can come back to saved plans, resources and simple next steps that match your current energy levels.

Open members dashboard Book a first conversation
  • Personalised notes and follow-ups from our charitable calls.
  • Simple, MS-friendly checklists instead of long documents.
  • Links to recommended tools already filtered for accessibility.
  • Space for carers and family to see the same plan (if you want).

This area will connect to your existing login via Supabase authentication. Access can be limited to people you explicitly invite.
